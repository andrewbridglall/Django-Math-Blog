About

Purpose of the blog

Hi, I'm Andrew! I previously took Analysis I with Dr. Der-Chen Chang in fall 2020. After covering over 120 theorems, defintions, postulates and remarks by the end of the semester, my handwritten study guides were a mess.

This blog contains every theorem covered in the course rendered in LaTeX. Hopefully it can serve as a helpful resource to review for homework and exams. For each chapter, there is also a downloadable pdf of the theorems. Along with a few trusted Analysis I classmates, I made sure that all theorems contained here read verbatim from the course text, An Introduction to Analysis (4th edition) by William R. Wade. If you do spot an error, let me know and I'll try to fix it as soon as possible.

Wade, W. R. (2009). An Introduction to Analysis (4th ed.). Prentice Hall.
